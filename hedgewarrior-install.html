Installation Steps
  1. Download MetaTrader 5 (MT5):
    Get the MT5 terminal from your broker‚Äôs official website in the Downloads section.
  2. Install MT5 on your computer:
    Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.
  3. Login to your trading account:
    Use the credentials provided by your broker to log in to your live or demo account.
  4. Copy Hedge Warrior EA to MT5:
    Place the Hedge Warrior V1 EA file (.ex5) in the MQL5/Experts folder of your MT5 terminal.
    • Open MetaTrader 5.
    • In MT5, go to File → Open Data Folder. This will open a file explorer window in the root of your MT5 data directory.
    • Inside the data folder, open the MQL5 subfolder.
    • Inside MQL5, open the Experts subfolder.
    • Copy the Hedge Warrior V1 .ex5 file and paste it into the Experts folder.
      (Full path: MQL5/Experts/ inside your MT5 data folder. Example: C:\Users\YourUser\AppData\Roaming\MetaQuotes\Terminal\long_id\MQL5\Experts\)
    • Close the file explorer window.
  5. Restart MT5 or Refresh Navigator:
    Restart MT5 or right-click the ‚ÄúNavigator‚Äù panel and select ‚ÄúRefresh‚Äù so the EA appears under "Expert Advisors".
  6. Attach EA to a BTCUSD Chart:
    • Open a BTCUSD chart in MT5.
    • In the Navigator panel (left side), expand "Expert Advisors", then drag and drop Hedge Warrior V1 onto the chart.
    • Make sure automated trading is enabled. Look at the top of your MT5 window for a ▶ green "AutoTrading" play button. If it is not green, click it once to enable. The button must be green for the EA to trade automatically.
  7. Check for Graduation Cap (🎓):
    Ensure a 🎓 blue graduation cap appears in the top-right corner of the chart, indicating the EA is running. (If not, automated trading may not be enabled.)
